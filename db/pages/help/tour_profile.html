Profile Tab

This is your profile page — the page all about you. Here you'll see your own most recent activity on the site, things you've shared, and notes that others have sent to you.

On the left, you'll find links to your family, groups, and other people you're keeping track of.

About You

Someone looking for you on the site will see this page mostly the same way you do. You can ensure your contact information and other details about you are up-to-date by clicking "Edit Profile." There you can upload a picture of yourself, change your mobile phone number, list your favorite books, and much more. Share as little or as much as you want.

Privacy

You can quickly and easily hide parts of your information by clicking "Privacy Settings."
